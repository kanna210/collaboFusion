About Us

Fusion Noodle Company is a family-owned Asian restaurant located in the heart of Athens, Ohio. Proudly serving the local community and Ohio University students, we specialize in fresh, flavorful dishes that bring people together. Fusion. Noodle Company offers a modern interpretation of Chinese, Vietnamese, and Asian Fusion dishes for dine-in, takeout, and delivery. As a partner of the 30 Mile meal movement, we serve unprocessed, made-from-scratch menu using the freshest ingredients from local farms. At Fusion Noodle Company, every meal is an invitation to enjoy great food, warm hospitality, and a welcoming atmosphere.

Yes we grow our own ingredients!

At Fusion Noodle Company, we believe great food starts with fresh, quality ingredients. That’s why we’ve taken the extra step to grow many of our own vegetables, including broccoli, right in our very own greenhouse. This allows us to bring you the freshest flavors possible while supporting sustainable practices. From farm to table, we’re proud to serve dishes made with care, love, and the bounty of our greenhouse. It’s just one of the ways we strive to make every meal special for the Athens community and Ohio University students.
